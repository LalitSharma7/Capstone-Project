Two way ANOVA testing¶

1) Relationship between column of interest i.e., selling_price and first categorical column i.e., owner

Ho:The type of owner does not affect the selling_price

H1:The type of owner does affect the seling_price

2) Relationship between column of interest i.e., selling_price and first categorical column i.e., seller_type

Ho:The seller_type does not affect the selling_price

H1:The seller_type does affect the selling_price

3) Relationship between column of interest i.e., selling_price and both the categorical columns i.e., owner and seller_type

Ho:The owner and seller_type does not affect the selling_price

H1:The owner and seller_type does affect the selling_price

In [49]:
from statsmodels.formula.api import ols
In [50]:
two_way=ols('selling_price~C(owner)+C(seller_type)+C(owner):C(seller_type)',data=df4).fit()
import statsmodels.api as sm
sm.stats.anova_lm(two_way)
