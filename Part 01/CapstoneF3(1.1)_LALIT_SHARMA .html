1.1 b) For describing the price of a car we can use correlation function between target column and numerical feature coulmn here if value is>0.7. Generally, a value of r greater than 0.7 is considered a strong correlation. Anything between 0.5 and 0.7 is a moderate correlation, and anything less than 0.4 is considered a weak or no correlation. In our analysis max_power is highly correlated whereas Km_driven and mileage negatively correlated.
